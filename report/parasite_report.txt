Parasite is a 2019 South Korean black comedy thriller film directed by Bong Joon-ho. The film follows the members of a poor family who scheme to become employed by a wealthy family by infiltrating their household and posing as unrelated, highly qualified individuals. As they become more entangled in the lives of the wealthy family, their deception becomes increasingly difficult to maintain, leading to a shocking and violent conclusion. The film explores themes of social inequality, class conflict, and the lengths to which people will go to improve their circumstances. Overall, Parasite is a masterful work of cinema that is both entertaining and thought-provoking.
